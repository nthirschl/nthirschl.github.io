About CV

I provide quantitative research and analytic support for organizations working on problems that matter—nonprofits, public agencies, academic researchers, and mission-driven businesses. Trained as a sociologist, I have experience analyzing administrative data, surveys, and public datasets across food systems, education, labor markets, and access to services. I typically work as an ongoing research partner rather than a one-off consultant, handling research design, data cleaning, statistical analysis, and interpretation. I also help organizations think through earlier-stage questions: what data to collect, how to collect it, and how analysis can inform strategy and decision-making. My aim is work that's methodologically sound but also genuinely useful—clear enough for non-specialists, honest about its limitations, and designed to support real decisions.

Services

  • Survey design and analysis
  • Literature review and evidence synthesis
  • Program evaluation and impact analysis
  • Statistical modeling and machine learning
  • Demographic analysis and projections
  • Mapping and geospatial analysis
  • Data cleaning, integration, and quality assessment
  • Data visualization and reporting
  • Measurement and metrics development
